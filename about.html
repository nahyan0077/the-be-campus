📅 2021 — The Beginning

Founded with a Mission to Change Kottakkal

Be Campus Academy was founded in Kottakkal, Malappuram with one unwavering goal — to give students in our district access to career-changing professional training. The founders saw a massive gap: students were graduating with degrees but no employable skills. Be Campus was built to fix that.

✦ Founded in Kottakkal ✦ First Batch Enrolled ✦ Career-First Curriculum
📅 2022 — Expansion

Launched 6 Advanced Diploma Programs

Responding to overwhelming demand and listening closely to industry hiring managers, Be Campus expanded to 6 professional diploma programs — covering Digital Marketing, Graphic Design, Office Management, HR, and Accounting. Suddenly, students from across Malappuram were making the journey to Kottakkal.

✦ 6 Diploma Courses ✦ Industry Tie-Ups ✦ Placement Cell
📅 2023 — The Game-Changer

Launched Be Cubez — Our Own Marketing Agency

This was the moment Be Campus became truly unique. We launched Be Cubez — our in-house digital marketing agency — giving Be Campus students the once-in-a-career opportunity to work on real client campaigns, real briefs, and real results while still in training. No other training institute in Malappuram offers this. Be Cubez turned our students from learners into practitioners.

✦ Be Cubez Agency Born ✦ Live Client Projects ✦ Real Portfolios Built
📅 2024 — AI Revolution

Became Malappuram's First AI-Integrated Training Centre

As AI began reshaping every industry, Be Campus acted fast. We rebuilt our curriculum from the ground up, integrating AI tools into every single program — from AI-powered SEO and ad tools in Digital Marketing, to AI accounting software in our finance courses. We became the first and only AI-integrated training institute in Malappuram district.

✦ AI Tools in All Courses ✦ First in Malappuram ✦ Future-Ready Training
📅 2025 — Recognition

500+ Students Placed. 5-Star Google Rating Achieved.

2025 marked the moment Be Campus became undeniably the best training institute in Kottakkal and Malappuram. Over 500 graduates placed across Kerala, India, and Gulf countries. A perfect 5-star Google rating built on genuine student reviews. And an ever-growing waiting list of students who want nothing else but Be Campus.

✦ 500+ Placed ✦ 5★ Google ✦ Gulf Placements
📅 2026 & Beyond — The Future

Building Kerala's #1 AI Career Training Hub

Our journey is nowhere near over. We're expanding programs, onboarding more industry partners, growing Be Cubez into a full-scale agency, and building toward our vision of being Kerala's most recognised career training institution. Every student we place is a chapter in this story — and we're just getting started.

✦ Kerala Expansion ✦ New Programs ✦ Be Cubez Scale-Up
